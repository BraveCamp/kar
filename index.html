Share Your Story

Tell us how you deal with racism

We want people to tell us their experiences and how they've dealt with racism. Gives us your background, how you've been affected and what the person said or did that seemed racist. Also tell us how you responded to the situation.

We wanted people to share their story's so they feel that they have a voice. Not only that but there are some people that feel like they are alone in the matter. What this could accomplish is give confidence to people who are to scared to speak up. We want everyone to have the confidence to change something.
